📊 Financial Analysis Dashboard

Risk and Return Measures

  • Beta of a stock relative to the market over a specified period
  • Alpha from the CAPM or multi-factor models
  • Volatility (standard deviation of returns)
  • Correlation between two or more assets

Performance Measures

  • Holding-period returns
  • Annualized returns
  • Excess returns over a benchmark
  • Sharpe ratio or Treynor ratio

Market and Firm Characteristics

  • Market capitalization
  • Price-to-earnings (P/E) ratio
  • Dividend yield
  • Book-to-market ratio

Time-Series or Cross-Sectional Analysis

  • Returns for a stock, index, or portfolio over a specified date range
  • Comparison of returns across multiple stocks or sectors
  • Simple portfolio returns based on selected asset weights
